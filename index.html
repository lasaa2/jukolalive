    Where can I watch Jukola Live?

    You can watch Jukola Live everywhere outside Finland. You cannot buy a stream access in Finland.

    How much does a Jukola Live access cost?

    Jukola Live access costs EUR 18 and it includes both, Jukola and Venla, relays in 2019. 

    Why do I need to Sign up?

    During the purchasing process, you create Jukola Live credentials for yourself. These credentials offer you an access to the stream player after the payment. 

    Can I use PayPal?

    Yes. We accept major credit cards and PayPal.

    When can I see the Jukola Live schedule?

    The schedule of Jukola Live broadcast is published on the Jukola Live website a couple of days before the event.
